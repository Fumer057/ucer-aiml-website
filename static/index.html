✦ About Us

Building the next generation of AI engineers

The UCER AIML Club is a student-run community at United College of Engineering and Research, dedicated to exploring and applying the latest in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

We host hands-on workshops, hackathons, and project competitions that bridge the gap between academic concepts and real-world industry skills. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there's a place for you here.

  • Weekly coding sessions & AI paper readings
  • Hackathons with industry-level problem statements
  • 1-on-1 mentorship from senior students & professionals
  • Access to GPU resources and premium tools
  • Internship & research referral network
